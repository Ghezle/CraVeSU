What is CraVeSU?

CraVeSU is the combination of words Crave and CVSU which means Cavite State University, is a student centered canteen and cafe hybrid designed to offer quaity, affordable meals in a cozy and stylish environment. With its playful name and welcoming vibe, CraVeSU delivers on it's tagline: Crave it, Love it, CraVeSU!.

This isn't your typical college cafeteria. CraVeSU brings a fresh, aesthetic twist to campus dining, blending modern design with a warm atmosphere—perfect for a quick bite, a group lunch, or even a chill study session over iced coffee or milk tea.

The Vibe: Aesthetic Meets Functionality

The exterior of CraVeSU features a minimalist beige facade accented by lush green landscaping, large windows,and a prominently displayed logo that's hard to miss. Inside, students enjoy an open layout and bright lighting, motivational banners, and a cozy yet efficient atmosphere.

What's on the Menu?

CraVeSu serves silog meals, rice bowls, pasta, finger foods, shakes, and more. It also plans to introduce healthy and vegetarian options for students seeking nutritious meals.
